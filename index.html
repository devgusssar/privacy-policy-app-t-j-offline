Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 21, 2025

Summary

  • We do not require registration and do not ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • We collect only technical and anonymous usage data — to improve the product and analyze stability.
  • We use third‑party SDKs for analytics.
  • The app may display banners (image, text, and link) that lead to third‑party sites offering goods/services. We do not show personalized ads inside the app.
  • If you email us, we use that information only to respond to your inquiry.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal data (full name, email, phone number, postal address) — unless you contact us directly.
  • Account data: we do not perform sign‑in and do not create user profiles.
  • User content, contacts, photos/videos, payment data — we do not request or store.

Examples (Non‑Exhaustive) of Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details (unless you email us).
  • We do not ask you to create an account and we do not perform authentication.
  • We do not read or store any data you enter in the app (if such fields exist), except for the technical metrics described below.

Data We Collect

The app collects anonymous usage metrics. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • How often the app is opened.
  • How often certain features of the app are used.

This information is not personally identifiable and is stored in analytics systems.

As you might expect, if you contact us directly, we will keep that information and may use it to improve the app.

How We Use Data

  • Analytics and product improvement: understand how the app is used, fix issues, and improve stability and quality.
  • Technical support: investigate incidents and respond to inquiries.
  • Non‑personal statistics: aggregated reports on feature usage and content effectiveness.

Tracking and App Tracking Transparency

We request permission to track in accordance with App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14.5+). You can disable tracking:

  • in the system prompt at first launch,
  • or later in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking (turn off “Allow Apps to Request to Track” or toggle it off for our app).

If you decline tracking, the app will continue to work, and collection will be limited to non‑personal analytics and diagnostics.

Ads and Partner Links

  • The app may display advertising/partner banners (image, text, and link) that lead to websites offering goods and services.
  • We do not share your personal data with advertisers. When you click a banner, the destination site may collect its own data under its policies.
  • We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy policies of third‑party sites. Please review their terms and policies before using them.

Sharing with Third Parties (Analytics Partners)

  • AppsFlyer — attribution of installs/campaigns and event analytics.
    Privacy Policy: https://www.appsflyer.com/legal/services-privacy-policy/
  • MyTracker — usage analytics and statistics.
    Privacy Policy: https://tracker.my.com/legal/privacy-policy/

These providers process the categories of data described above under their own policies and may use standard device/advertising identifiers. We do not sell your data.

Storage and Security

  • We apply organizational and technical measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction of data.
  • Data is retained for as long as necessary for the purposes described above and/or according to retention periods set by our analytics providers and the law.
  • Transmission over the internet cannot be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can request information about the data we process and ask for its deletion, where technically feasible and not contrary to the law or our obligations to third parties (e.g., analytics providers).
  • You can limit analytics collection at the device level (for example, by resetting or limiting the advertising identifier in your OS settings).

Age Limits

The app is intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data about children under 13. If you become aware that a child’s data has been provided to us, please contact us — we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The new version takes effect upon publication. The last updated date is shown above.
